Untitled IV

2023 Mixed media

A mixed media piece combining found materials with paint and ink. The layering process creates depth and texture that invites closer inspection.

Materials

This piece incorporates:

  • Acrylic paint
  • India ink
  • Newspaper clippings
  • Handmade paper

Process

The work began as a series of ink washes on handmade paper, onto which I collaged fragments of newspaper text. The final layers of acrylic paint partially obscure the text, creating a dialogue between the visible and the hidden.

The tension between concealment and revelation is central to this piece — words peek through paint, meaning dissolves into form.
