Untitled III

2023 Digital illustration

A series of digital illustrations exploring the intersection of organic forms and geometric precision.

Concept

This piece began as a study of natural patterns — the branching of trees, the spiral of shells — reinterpreted through a strict digital grid. The tension between the two systems became the subject itself.

Process

Working in layers, I started with scanned ink drawings and progressively transformed them through algorithmic distortion. Each pass stripped away some of the hand-drawn quality while introducing new symmetries.

The final composition retains just enough of the original gesture to feel alive, while the underlying structure holds it in place.

Technical Notes

  • Created using a combination of hand drawing and generative software
  • Final output printed at 60 × 80 cm on archival matte paper
  • Edition of 5
