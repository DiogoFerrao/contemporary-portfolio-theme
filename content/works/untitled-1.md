This piece explores the tension between structure and organic form. Painted over the course of several weeks, the layered oils create a depth that shifts depending on the light in the room.

## Process

The work began as a series of quick gestural sketches, which were then translated onto a large-format canvas. Each layer was allowed to dry fully before the next was applied, building up a rich surface texture.

## Dimensions

120 × 80 cm
