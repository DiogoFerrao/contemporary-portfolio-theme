A photographic study exploring light, shadow, and the quiet drama of everyday spaces.

## Concept

This work began as a series of walks through the city at dusk, capturing the moments where natural light gives way to artificial glow. The resulting images sit in a liminal space — neither fully day nor night.

## Process

Shot on medium format film over the course of several weeks. Each frame was chosen for its sense of stillness and the way light pooled in unexpected corners.

> "Photography is about finding something interesting in an ordinary place. I've found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them." — Elliott Erwitt
